Minimal Latency

Timing is everything for the perfect live performance, which makes latency the audio industry's worst enemy. Sheerlink gives extreme low-latency. Less than 2ms depending on configuration.

Zero Interference

With intelligent radio drivers and optionally using reserved radio band Sheerlink eliminates drop-outs and creates hyper-smooth audio delivery. A robust wireless link bridges instruments or microphones and receivers with a lightning fast connection with a quality, unique in the music industry.

Zero Wires

Designing a new wireless standard

Sheerlink introduces a new standard in wireless audio performance for professionals that truly moves the boundaries for what can be expected from radio performance and reliability. Sheerlink is designed for perfect co-existence in the musical environment. Sheerlink creates a super strong transmitting highway with a robust radio frequency transmission.

On top of that Sheerlink is built around the Sheersound audio codec by RTX. Sheersound is designed for audio perfection, where the highest possible audio quality seamlessly blends with extremely low latency.
